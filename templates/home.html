Methodology:

Heat Factor takes all of the GM, M, A and B class shooters (sorry C and D class, you need to dry fire more) in a
division and assigns a random rating within a range to each shooter.

The ranges are:
GM: 95-100
M: 85-94
A: 75-84
B: 60-74

Then sum the total of the ratings and divide by the total number of GM, M, A and B class shooters in the division.
