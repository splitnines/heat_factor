Methodology:

The Heat Factor App collects the classification information from the Match Breakdown section of a Practiscore.com match URL. Each shooter is then given a rank based on thier classification.
The rankings that are used align with USPSA classifications:
GM: 95-100
M: 85-94
A: 75-84
B: 60-74
C, D, U and X class shooters are omitted from the rankings

The rank assigned to a shooter is randomly choosen from within the classification range. For examle, an "M" class shooter can receive a rank within the 85-94 range. This random assignment will cause the results to shift slightly each time the app is run for a given match.
Once the ranks have been assigned they are totaled per each division and divided by the total number of GM, M, A and B class shooters in the division. This give the average classification of those shooters and the final results of the Heat Factor.
