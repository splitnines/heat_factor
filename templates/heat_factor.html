Methodology:

The Heat Factor app collects the classification information from the Match Breakdown section of a Practiscore.com match URL. Each shooter is then given a rank based on thier classification.
The rankings that are used align with USPSA classifications:

GM: 100
M: 85
A: 75

B, C, D, U and X class shooters are omitted from the rankings

Once the ranks have been assigned they are totaled for each division and divided by the total number of GM, M, and A class shooters in the division.
This gives the average classification of those shooters and the final results of the Heat Factor.
